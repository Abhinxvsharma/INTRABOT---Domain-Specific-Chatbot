# Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave must be approved by the manager at least 2 weeks in advance.

## Sick Leave
Employees are allowed 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 12 weeks of paid maternity leave. This can be extended by up to 4 weeks based on medical advice.

## Notice Period
The notice period for termination of employment by either party is 30 days, unless otherwise specified in the employment contract.
